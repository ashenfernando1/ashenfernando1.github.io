Iron filings/magnets demo

Background and Instructions

Concepts: Ferromagnetism, magnetic field lines

Iron filings map out the magnetic field lines of a magnet

Sprinkle iron filings onto board and show how they naturally line up with the magnetic field lines of the magnet (which is glued in the middle of the board)

Equipment

  • 1 magnet/board set-up
