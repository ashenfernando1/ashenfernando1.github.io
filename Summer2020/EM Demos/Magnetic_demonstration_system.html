Magnetic demonstration system

Background and Instructions

Concepts: Magnetic induction, Eddy currents, Diamagnetism, Paramagnetism

Demonstrate eddy currents and how materials which exhibit different types of magnetism behave in a magnetic field

Swing pendula between variable gap magnet to show effects of eddy currents. Hang glass rod between variable gap magnet to show how a diamagnetic material reacts to a magnetic field (it counter-aligns). Hang the aluminum tube to show how a paramagnetic material acts in a magnetic field (it aligns)

Equipment

  • 1 PASCO EM-8641 variable gap magnet
  • 1 ring stand with grooved rod for attaching metal
  • 3 metal pendula (solid, slotted, corrugated)
  • 1 ssmall glass rod tied to thread
  • 1 small aluminum rod tied to thread

Aside from hanging the pendula, one coule also let the students hold them and feel for themselves the eddy current resistance as they pass it through the magnet
