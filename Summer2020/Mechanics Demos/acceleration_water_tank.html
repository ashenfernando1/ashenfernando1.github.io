Acceleration water tank

Background and Instructions

Concepts: Acceleration, inertia, centripetal force

Spinning a water tank shows the water surface (meniscus) become parabolic under the constant centripetal acceleration

A photogate is attached to measure the rate of spinning for the major axis. Another photogate is hooked up to the pulley, to which a mass hanger and string could be attached to unwind the tank, if needed

Equipment

  • 1 Acceleration water tank demo
  • 1+ liters of water
