Density experiment

Background and Instructions

Concepts: Sound waves

Speakers play at adjustable frequencies, can easily show beats. Couple well with resonance tube, of which there is more than one type.

Plug in and turn on sine wave generators, then adjust the dials to the desired frequencies. Hold resonance tube up to one speaker and adjust it to find resonant frequency.

Equipment

  • 1 Sound wave demo setup
  • 1 PASCO resonance tube
