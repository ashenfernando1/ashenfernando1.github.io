Mechanical/Electrical equivalent of heat

Background and Instructions

Concepts: Conservation of energy

Resistance bulb generates heat, heating up water

Fill container with 200ml water, insert bulb and temperature bulb, and watch temperature of water increase or plot power as work is done.

Equipment

  • 1 PASCO hand crank generator
  • 1 PASCO electric equivalent of heat container
  • 1 Xplore GLX
  • 1 temperature sensor
  • 200ml water

Note that water heats up very slowly, and it takes a disproportionate amount of energy to turn the crank. Also, the temperature sensor may move into cooler or warmer parts of the container due to jostling, giving bad data.
