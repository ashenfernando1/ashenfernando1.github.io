Tuning forks in sounding boxes

Background and Instructions

Concepts: Resonance, longitudinal waves, beats, frequencies

Strike tuning forks with mallet to show different vibrational phenomena. Adjust height of or remove metal pieces to show change in pitch. Strike one and put boxes together to show sympathetic harmonic.

Equipment

  • 2 tuning forks in sounding boxes
  • 1 rubber mallet
  • 2 adjustable metal pieces
