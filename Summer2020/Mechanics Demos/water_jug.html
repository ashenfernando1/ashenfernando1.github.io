Water jug demo

Background and Instructions

Concepts: Pressure differences

Holes at different points in a water jug show how water will drain to reach equilibirum pressure

Fill with water, remove rubber stoppers as necessary to show how water reaches equilibrium pressure. If dropped while draining into a container, it will be demonstrated that water does not have sufficient pressure to keep pouring out during freefall.

Equipment

  • 1 Water jug demo (with rubber stoppers)
